*You don't have to go full Haskell.*

## Planned Topics

- A maturity model for adopting FP in existing codebases:
  - Level 1: Purity and immutability as defaults — immediate payoff, low risk
  - Level 2: ADTs and exhaustive matching for domain modeling — moderate investment, high payoff
  - Level 3: Monadic error handling (Result/Either pipelines) — requires team buy-in
  - Level 4: Effect management (Reader, reactive pipelines) — architectural shift
  - Level 5: Full algebraic effect systems — for teams ready to invest heavily
- Where diminishing returns kick in for most teams
- Language-specific recommendations: what's idiomatic and well-supported in each language vs. what's fighting the grain
- The "functional core, imperative shell" as the universal starting point
